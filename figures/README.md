# Figures

## `published/`

`figure1.png` ... `figure8.png` are the figures **exactly as they appear in the
published manuscript** (byte-identical to the submitted document).

These were produced by the authors' own final plotting code, which is **not
included in this deposit**. They are provided here so that the deposit contains the
authoritative published artwork.

## `script_output/`

Output of `scripts/make_figures.py` and `scripts/make_figures2.py`. These scripts
regenerate plots **from the deposited data**, and they reproduce the underlying
numbers faithfully, but their styling and, in several cases, their choice of plotted
quantity differ from the published figures. In particular `figB_crossing.png` plots
the normal field against voltage, whereas published Figure 2 plots the dimensionless
electric capillary number Ca_E against voltage.

Use `published/` for the figures of record. Use `script_output/` to verify that the
plotted data derive from the deposited results.

To make this deposit fully self-reproducing, the authors' final figure scripts should
be added here before the archive is minted.
